About Pramukh Arista

SPREADING HAPPINESS WITH BLESSINGS

With the blessings of revered saints, we at PSY Group are committed to bring eternal blessings to lives of all. And with this unflinching dedication, we are spreading our footprints in and around Gandhinagar. In over 30 years of rich experience in the real estate development, we have completed 50+ projects in such way that the have earned us trust of more 3400 families.

Pramukh Arista is yet another milestone in our journey towards excellence.

Perched in a serene, calm surroundings of Sargasan, near S.G. Highway, Pramukh Arista is designed to create an epitome of grand living. Realize your dreams and watch new avenues of happiness unfolding before you.
